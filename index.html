Short Summary

As a Python developer and data analyst, I have honed my skills over the past 5+ years by working on a variety of projects that have allowed me to develop my technical abilities and expertise in the field.

I began my career as a Python developer, working on developing web applications for small and medium-sized businesses. Later realized that my passion for problem-solving and my ability to think critically could be further developed by combining it with data analysis.

I started to learn more about data analysis and visualization tools like Power Bi, Tableau, Pandas and NumPy. This led me to pursue more opportunities in data analysis and business intelligence

As I progressed in my career, I found my passion for both data analysis and software development, which led me to take on more projects that required me to combine both skills. I took on a role as a Data Analyst and Python developer, where I was responsible for both the development and maintenance of web applications, as well as the analysis and interpretation of data.
